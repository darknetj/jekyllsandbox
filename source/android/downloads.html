Note that Firefox and Firefox-based browsers will mangle the tarball downloads by adding an extra layer of gzip compression. This bug was reported to Mozilla in 2010 but has yet to be fixed. Either use a sane browser/tool to perform the download or replace the tar.gz extension with tar.gz.gz and remove the extra layer of compression with gunzip. We have a workaround in place on our end but OpenShift has a separate bug preventing it from working. Software sucks.
