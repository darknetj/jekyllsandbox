About

Copperhead Security specializes in protecting systems and devices from unauthorized access by helping companies secure their technical infrastucture.

Our team also provides certified forensics experts and investigative services to corporations and law firms. We respond to incidents involving data theft or hacking, including malware detection and mitigation. We also help lawyers make sense of complex legal cases involving digital forensics.

We sell hardened smartphones to organizations who need to keep their communications safe, using a secure version of Android that we are building, entirely open-source.

Located in Toronto, Canada.
